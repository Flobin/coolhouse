<section class="text">
<div class="leftcolumn">
    <p>
        In the past years, harbor locations have become increasingly popular as residential areas, because of their unique qualities. The old view of a harbor as purely a working environment has been transformed to a lively neighborhood for living, recreation, and relaxation. The water, quay, fishing boats, and yachts give the harbor a romantic character.
    </p>
    <p>
        The Scheveningen harbor is currently being transformed—not only are new living spaces being built, but also other features that will benefit future residents. Already, a supermarket, a bank, various restaurants and cafés, and the Zuiderstrand theater are within walking distance.
    </p>
</div>
<div class="rightcolumn">
    <p>
        With the Scheveningen beach and boulevard on one side of the harbor, and the southern beach on the other, this is the dream location of every sea and beach lover.
    </p>
    <p>
        The popular shopping street Frederik Hendriklaan is within a stone’s throw of the harbor.
    </p>
</div>
</section>
<h3 class="pullquote">‘The traditional image of the harbor as an area strictly for working will be transformed into a lively neighborhood for living, recreation, and relaxing.’</h3>
